Northern Lights Gallery

We know that many of the guests living in our cabins for rent are here to get a glimpse of the Northern Lights. But, what is the so called Northern Lights? It is a phenomenon called Aurora Borealis. The incredible light show is caused by collisions between electrically charged particles. These particles have been released by the sun, and collide with gases in our atmosphere.

We cannot guarantee that the Northern Lights will occur during your stay, but if you want to have a real shot at getting a glimpse of it, you should check out our cabins for rent!
